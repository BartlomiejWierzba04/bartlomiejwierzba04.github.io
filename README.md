# Privacy Policy for Osiris Plant Tracker

**Last Updated:** May 6, 2026

Welcome to **Osiris Plant Tracker** ("Osiris", "we", "our", or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is collected, used, and protected when you use our mobile application.

## 1. Information We Collect and How We Use It

Osiris operates primarily as a localized, offline-first application. We prioritize your privacy by keeping as much data on your personal device as possible.

### A. Location Data
- **What we collect:** With your explicit permission, Osiris collects your device's precise or approximate location (GPS data).
- **Why we collect it:** We use this data strictly to accurately plot your gardens/locations on the in-app map and to fetch accurate, real-time localized weather data for your plants.
- **Is it shared?:** Location data is transmitted securely to third-party weather APIs (such as Open-Meteo) strictly for retrieving weather forecasts. It is **not** stored on any remote servers managed by us, nor is it sold or shared for advertising.

### B. User-Generated Data
- **What we collect:** Information about the plants you track, custom nicknames, planting environments, watering schedules, and location names.
- **How it is stored:** All user-generated data is stored locally on your device's internal storage. We do not have access to this data, and we do not upload it to any external cloud servers. 

### C. Network and API Data
- **What we access:** Osiris requires internet access to fetch the open-source plant database and to request weather updates from our weather provider. 

## 2. Third-Party Services

We may employ third-party services to facilitate our application. These services may have access to your IP address and Location data strictly to perform tasks on our behalf:
* **Open-Meteo:** Used to fetch accurate weather and precipitation forecasts for your plants.
* **Expo:** Our application framework may collect basic crash reports or anonymized usage analytics to help us improve app stability.

## 3. Data Retention and Deletion

Because Osiris stores your personal plant and garden data locally on your device, you have full control over your data. 
- **Data Deletion:** You can permanently delete all your data at any time simply by uninstalling the application or clearing the app's data in your device's system settings. 

## 4. Children's Privacy

Our application does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. Because the app does not require user registration or accounts, no personal identity profiles are created.

## 5. Security

We value your trust in using our app. Since your data is kept locally on your device, its security relies on your device's built-in security features. We transmit location data to weather providers using standard, secure HTTPS protocols.

## 6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

## 7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us.
